This personal project showcases three out of ten doll concepts I created. Each doll represents a planet in our solar system. I researched to see what doll trends were popular and designed my dolls accordingly. Each doll concept took about a week to bring to fruition. The dolls and packaging concept were designed in Adobe illustrator.
